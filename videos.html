Inspired by Nicholas Harris' fantastic 2025 essay on the subject, I wanted to make a list of my favourite YouTube videos.

A segment from the Apollo documentary For All Mankind (1989). It's amazing footage and a really emotional interview with one of the Apollo astronauts, but the documentary as a whole is annoying because it splices together Gemini and Apollo missions almost at random.

Denis Healey and Roger Moore on the Dame Edna Christmas special. Not only is this a totally insane move by Healy, who only resigned from the shadow cabinet a few months before, but he is also being absolutely mogged by Moore.
